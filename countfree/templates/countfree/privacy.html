Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Summary

CountFree does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your countdown data stays on your device.

Data Collection

CountFree does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics services, and does not contain advertising SDKs.

All event data (names, dates, icons, themes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and shared with the widget extension via an App Group container. This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

CountFree offers optional tips and a supporter subscription through Apple's StoreKit. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple. CountFree does not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or Apple ID data.

Third-Party Services

CountFree does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app makes no network requests other than those required by Apple for StoreKit transaction processing.

Data Sharing

CountFree does not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share.

Children's Privacy

CountFree does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since CountFree collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact mckriel@gmail.com.
